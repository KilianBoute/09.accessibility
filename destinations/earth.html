EARTH

Earth's surface is incredibly diverse, encompassing continents, oceans, and a wide range of landscapes. Approximately 71% of the Earth's surface is covered by oceans, which host a rich variety of marine life. The remaining 29% is land, featuring vast plains, towering mountains, lush forests, and arid deserts. Earth's surface is the only known place in the universe where life thrives, thanks to its moderate climate and the presence of liquid water, making it a unique and dynamic planet in our solar system.
