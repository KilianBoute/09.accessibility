NEPTUNE

The surface of Neptune is primarily composed of gas, primarily hydrogen and helium, and is not solid like Earth's or Mars' surfaces. Neptune is often referred to as an "ice giant" due to the presence of icy and slushy materials beneath its atmosphere. It lacks a well-defined, solid surface that we can stand on, making it a very different world from our terrestrial planets.
